# Synthesis of Tungsten Oxide (WO₃) Nanoparticles by Hydrothermal Method

## Aim
To synthesize tungsten oxide (WO₃) nanoparticles using the hydrothermal method.

---

## Materials Required

### Chemicals
- Sodium tungstate (Na₂WO₄)  
- Double distilled water  
- Nitric acid (HNO₃)  
- Acetone  

### Apparatus Required
- Beaker  
- Conical flask  
- Measuring cylinder  
- Burette  
- Thermometer  
- Magnetic stirrer  
- Autoclave  
- Hot air oven  

---

## Formula

\[
W = N \times e \times l
\]

Where:
- **N** = Normality  
- **e** = Equivalent weight  
- **l** = Volume (in liters)  

---

## Procedure

1. **Preparation of Solution**  
   Dissolve 1.95 g of sodium tungstate (Na₂WO₄) in 30 ml of double distilled water.

2. **Stirring Process**  
   Stir the solution continuously until a uniform green-colored solution is obtained.

3. **Transfer to Autoclave**  
   After 10 minutes of stirring, transfer the solution into a 100 ml autoclave.

4. **Hydrothermal Treatment**  
   Place the autoclave in a hot air oven at 160°C for 24 hours.

5. **Washing Process**  
   Remove the product and wash several times with double distilled water and acetone.

6. **Acid Treatment**  
   Stir the obtained product in 30 ml of nitric acid (HNO₃) to form a yellow precipitate.

7. **Repeated Washing**  
   Wash the yellow precipitate repeatedly with acetone and distilled water.

8. **Calcination**  
   Calcine the precipitate at 500°C for 5 hours to obtain WO₃ nanoparticles.
